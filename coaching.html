How We Work

Nobody hands you a playbook when you go independent.

You became a creator journalist because you love the work. You wanted to report, create, do work that matters, own it and feel agency over your own career.

But now you're also running a business. Odds are, nobody taught you how to do that part. And now you're exhausted from figuring it all out alone, second-guessing yourself and shouldering everything.

Project C's coaching gives you a senior-level thought partner to help you make decisions, gain confidence, learn key skills and figure out how to make it all sustainable. It is the launchpad for you becoming the founder your work deserves.

Your Coach

Blair Hickman

Blair is a people-first media executive, strategist and coach who spent nearly two decades leading innovation, transformation and strategic growth inside fast-growing newsrooms like Vox and ProPublica. She's designed content strategies that reached hundreds of millions of people, led strategic planning and brand refreshes, and built Vox's consumer revenue program from the ground up.

She's an expert in design and product thinking, and believes deeply in building a business that can support your life. She's a co-founder of Going Solo, and at this point has coached nearly 100 creator journalists on launching and sustaining independent businesses.

You'll also get occasional sessions with Project C founder Liz Kelly Nelson, who led innovation at The Washington Post and Vox before building the 200+ member Project C community and The Independent Journalism Atlas. Her ongoing research into how journalism and the creator economy intersect informs everything we do here.

Between the two of them, you're working with people who've actually built the kinds of things you're trying to build.

What Coaching Looks Like

What we work on together

Our style blends intuitive coaching, teaching and strategic advisory. You lead the sessions, and we adapt as you grow. We prioritize action, iteration and sustainable progress.

🎯

Ruthlessly prioritize

Stop chasing six different ideas and get clear on the one thing that moves your business forward right now.

📈

Grow your audience and revenue

Look at your business model with fresh eyes. Find the opportunities you're too close to see and build a realistic plan to go after them.

⚙️

Build systems that don't depend on you

Create processes that keep things running even when you step away. Get your operations running in a way that's actually sustainable.

💪

Gain confidence to lead

Whether you're managing contractors or doing everything yourself, learn to lead your business instead of just reacting to it.

💡

Deal with the burnout

Learn to break big ambitions into steps you can actually take. Create space for the parts of your life that aren't work. Stay in this for the long haul.

🎨

Experiment and play

Not every session is about optimizing. Sometimes the most useful thing is permission to try something weird and see what happens.
