Meet the cohort

Muslim Creator Journalism Accelerator Spring 2026

Sixteen Muslim creator-journalists from across the U.S., building sustainable, audience-supported journalism on their own platforms. They are the Spring 2026 cohort of the Muslim Creator Journalism Accelerator. Tap any link below to follow their work, and save this page to reconnect after the event.

01

Ameer Al-Khatahtbeh

New York, NY · Muslim (@muslim)

Founder of @muslim, the largest online platform for Muslim voices with an audience of more than six million. Now focused on building a full media institution for young Muslims, with a team and a complete publication.

muslim.co Instagram TikTok YouTube Personal site
02

Rabia Chaudry

Bowie, MD · Undisclosed

Attorney, best-selling author of Adnan's Story, and host of the hit podcast Undisclosed. Covers criminal justice and wrongful convictions, and is already profitable as she expands deeper into creator journalism.

Website Podcast Instagram
03

Jennifer Chowdhury

Queens, NY · Port of Entry

Jennifer Chowdhury is an award-winning journalist, former foreign correspondent, and founder of Port of Entry, a multimedia publication covering immigration and diaspora life and politics across South Asian and Muslim America with an intergenerational and intersectional lens. Her work has appeared in the Guardian, the New York Times, the Washington Post, NPR, and more.

Newsletter Website Instagram TikTok
04

Ghada Dajani

Brooklyn, NY · Hadhi Ghada

Documents Arab hospitality and food culture on Instagram as Hadhi Ghada, weaving Palestinian cultural documentation with gathering as an answer to loneliness. Growing toward monetization through workshops and events.

Links coming soon

05

Yasmine El-Sabawi

Washington, DC · Middle East Eye

Veteran journalist with more than a decade in the field and the only DC correspondent for Middle East Eye. Covers the Muslim diaspora and immigration policy while building an independent platform of her own.

Website Instagram Bluesky
06

Farah Eltohamy

Seattle, WA · Independent

Investigative journalist building a publication covering Muslim-American communities and immigration detention. Previously reported for Cascade PBS and the Texas Tribune.

Website Bluesky
07

Tahirah Folk

Queens, NY · Sincerely Tahiry

TikTok creator and cultural producer whose work centers Black Muslim women across culture, identity, and history. A multi-hyphenate researcher and writer with a strong intersectional lens, publishing as Sincerely Tahiry.

Newsletter TikTok Instagram YouTube
08

Sahar Habib Ghazi

Berkeley, CA · Pink Salt

Veteran journalist and former Global Voices managing editor writing Pink Salt, a newsletter on Muslim parenting, intergenerational healing, and mental health. A clear voice in an underserved niche, with a podcast to match.

Newsletter Instagram
09

Zineb Haddaji

Phoenix, AZ · Independent

Independent creator journalist covering social issues, human rights, migration, and press freedom, with a focus on Djibouti. She publishes content in French and English on Instagram and TikTok, bringing a Djiboutian perspective to regional and global issues.

Instagram TikTok
10

Ghazala Irshad

Chicago, IL · The Brown Line

Her media platform The Brown Line covers Chicago arts, culture, and community through a Global South diaspora lens, spanning a newsletter and social video storytelling. Already monetizing, with deep ties across the city's neighborhoods, she is a model for what local creator journalism can look like.

Website Instagram
11

Rahim Jessani

Boston, MA · Bottom Up

Covers hard news for TikTok, from national politics to local Boston stories to international affairs, with a civics-first approach. His newsletter Bottom Up extends the reporting.

Newsletter TikTok Instagram YouTube
12

Husam Kaid

Brooklyn, NY · Independent

Covers Middle East geopolitics and New York politics on Instagram and YouTube, where he co-hosts a podcast.

Links coming soon

13

Hussain Khan

Oakland, CA · H.M. Khan on Substack

UC Berkeley-trained journalist who left the traditional newsroom to go independent. His Substack covers politics and grassroots movements alongside faith and U.S.-Pakistan relations.

Newsletter
14

Syeda Khaula Saad

Jersey City, NJ · Brown Lotus

Writes Brown Lotus, covering sexual health and wellness, relationships, and identity from a Desi Muslim woman perspective. Brings a journalism background and a master's degree to topics often underserved in South Asian media.

Newsletter Instagram
15

Karim Shamsi-Basha

Birmingham, AL · Writer / Photographer

Veteran photographer, writer, and speaker with more than 30 years of published work and an award-winning book list. Writes about life, culture, spirituality, and food from a Southern Muslim perspective. Creator work is already his main income, making him the most established member of the cohort.

Links coming soon

16

Zaineb Sharif

Washington, DC · Independent / Broadcast

Mid-Atlantic Emmy-winning TV journalist who has reported for Al Jazeera English, NBC, and CBS. Covers U.S. politics and international affairs as they affect Muslim communities, and is building a creator platform alongside her broadcast work.
