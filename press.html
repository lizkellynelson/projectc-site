Research

Independent journalism didn’t disappear. It moved.

So we track where it went: who’s doing the work, how they’re funding it, and what’s keeping most of them from breaking even. We partner with researchers and newsrooms to put real numbers behind a shift everyone can feel but few have measured. The reports below are where we show that work.

With CNTI · April 2026 · The latest

US Indie Info Providers: Professionally Diverse, Mission-Driven, Rarely Earning Profit

A first look at the people shaping independent information in the United States, and the challenges keeping most of them from breaking even. Project C contributed recruitment, field knowledge, and expertise to this landmark study by the Center for News, Technology & Innovation.
