How to Save & Use Your Card

  1. 1Click Download above to save an HTML file to your computer. Open it in Chrome or Safari.
  2. 2In your browser, go to File → Print (or ⌘P / Ctrl+P). Make sure "Background graphics" is checked so the dark card background prints correctly.
  3. 3Choose "Save as PDF" as your destination to get a PDF file, or select your printer to print directly. Set scaling to Actual Size.
  4. 4To share digitally (e.g. for an event application), attach the PDF or take a screenshot of the card and crop to size.

💳 Want a physical card? Bring your PDF to FedEx Office, Staples, or a local print shop and ask for it printed on card stock. For a truly professional finish, ask about lamination — it makes the card look and feel like a real press credential. Standard business card size (3.5" × 2") works perfectly.
